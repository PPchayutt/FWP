Top 4 Italian brainrot character

Italian brainrot is a series of Internet memes that emerged in early 2025 characterized by surrealist and absurd images of
AI-generated creatures who are given pseudo-Italian names. The phenomenon quickly spread across social media platforms such as TikTok and Instagram,
going viral owing to its combination of sloppy aesthetics, synthesized "Italian" voiceovers, grotesque or humorous visuals, abstractism, and nonsensical narrative.

Tralalero Tralala

The first viral character of the genre - a three-"legged" shark in Nike sneakers described as athletic, being able to run at superhuman speeds and jump high.

Bombardiro Crocodilo

A hybrid creature with the head of a crocodile and the body of a World War II-era twin-prop bomber.

Tung Tung Tung Sahur

An anthropomorphic wooden plank who holds a baseball bat. Although considered part of Italian brainrot, it has Indonesian origin.

Lirili Larila

A bipedal cactus-elephant hybrid wearing sandals who has the ability to control time.
